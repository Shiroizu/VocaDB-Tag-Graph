#Genres

>Hardcore spin-off with diminished speed (140-150 BPM). Loud claps are layered over the kicks, thus creating a stomping beat that encourages to jump to it. Bass and melody are usually arranged in a shuffle beat, which supports the stomping character. (from Discogs)
