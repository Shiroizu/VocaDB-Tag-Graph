#Vocalists

_Open JTalk_ is a free text-to-speech synthesis software developed by the [Nagoya Institute of Technology](https://vocadb.net/Ar/117172). 
Four characters exist within the program:
* [m001](https://vocadb.net/Ar/117267), the original voicebank for the program
* [Mei](https://vocadb.net/Ar/115444), one of the mascots for the [Nagoya Institute of Technology](https://vocadb.net/Ar/117172); she was released on May 1st, 2011
* [tohoku-f01](https://vocadb.net/Ar/117277), a vocal representing Tohoku University; she was released on [Dec 20, 2015](https://github.com/icn-lab/htsvoice-tohoku-f01)
* Takumi, the other mascot for Nagoya; he was released on [Dec 25,](https://sourceforge.net/projects/open-jtalk/files/Open%20JTalk/open_jtalk-1.11/) [2018](https://sourceforge.net/p/mmdagent/code/HEAD/tree/MMDAgent_Example/Voice/)
