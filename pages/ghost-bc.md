#Copyrights

Ghost B.C., formally known as Ghost, is a Swedish rock & metal band with a focus on theatricality, religion, and satanic imagery. All members aside from lead vocalist Tobias Forge are referred to as "Nameless Ghouls". Forge often plays the part of the different eras of religious leader characters such as Papa Emiritus IV and Cardinal Copia.
