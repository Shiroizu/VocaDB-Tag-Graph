#Series

The **PANDORA VOXX** Project is a Vocaloid song series created by **Kemu**, and was later adapted into novels.
There are currently 10 main songs.

>A wish-granting device, presumably named the PANDORA VOXX, was created six trillion years and one night before Riku's story. This supernatural device appears as a small box, sometimes depicted with extra details. Maki and the device are in some form one and the same, with Maki being a "wind-up".

___
