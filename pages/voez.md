#Games

VOEZ is a rhythm game by Rayark Games developed for iOS, Android and Nintendo Switch.
It features many VOCALOID and UTAU songs.
Available in English, Japanese and Korean.

Release dates:

- iOS:
WW - 26 May 2016
- Android:
WW - 2 June 2016
- Nintendo Switch:
JP - 3 March 2017
PAL - 3 March 2017
NA - 9 March 2017
