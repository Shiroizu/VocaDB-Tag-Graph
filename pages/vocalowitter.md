#Vocalists

An iPhone app that allows the user to create short phrases with Vocaloid synthesis engine by entering words, adjusting the sounds' length and pitch and applying various intonations.

The resulting sequence can be exported to audio file and sent via email, uploaded to YouTube or published at Twitter.

Currently discontinued.
