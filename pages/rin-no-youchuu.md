#Animation

Rin no Youchuu (リンの幼虫, *Larval Rin*) is a derivative of [Kagamine Rin](http://vocadb.net/Ar/14). It was conceptualized as a sort of pre-evolution of Rin. The creator of Rin no Youchuu is Mossan.
